Who am I?

Autumn is a motivated software developer with a fullstack software education. She recently graduated from Cal Poly Humboldt with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and is excited to gain experience working outside of education and begin navigating the complex challenges of real-world product development and software maintenance.

Since gradution Autumn has devoted herself to mastering diverse coding languages and staying up to date with software development research and techniques. Ultimately she would like to earn a master's degree, and until that time comes she aims to stay competitive, contribute to existing software, and advance her career through professional experience.

Outside of working on her personal stretch project, Autumn enjoys working with her hands. She is often researching gardening topics, seeking new crochet projects, and looking for new meals to attempt in the kitchen. Once she has the means to attend further education, she would like to attend courses in Construction Technology to gain useful skills in home improvement.
